About Me

Hello and welcome to my webpage. Here you can explore the work I have done as a full stack web developer. Web development is a hobby for me at the moment, but I would love to turn in into a career.

My path to becoming a Web Developer has been a circuitous one. I have worked in a variety of fields, from security to social services. I have 8 year professional experience, which has provided me with many helpful skills such as communication, collaboration, and time management. I have a BA in economics from Lewis & Clark College and a MDiv from Naropa University. I love to learn new things and think about the mechanics and motivations behind things. I am an excellent problem solver and creative thinker.

I live in South Denver and my hobbies include reading, writing, and hiking.
